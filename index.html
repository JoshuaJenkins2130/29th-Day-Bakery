What is Honey Holler?

After years of running a household and baking pies that will bring a grown man to his knees, we're finally turning pro. Our new bakery, Honey Holler, is where apron meets ambition- and where calories don't count if you eat standing up. What started as "just one for husband's work, just one for a friend" has somehow snowballed into a small-town want. Our secret? BUTTER. And plenty of it.
