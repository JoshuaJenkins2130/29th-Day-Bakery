Behind the Pie

Born in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains, Tawny was raised in the hills of Harlan, KY. Since a young girl, she spent many hours climbing chairs and counter tops helping Mamaw crack the eggs and bake the pie's.

Over the last decade, Tawny has served as an Army Wife, Kentucky Educator as a Christian County Colonel, and stay at home momma to a beautiful girl, boy, and German Shepherd.

She now resides on a small farm in Western Kentucky, continuing her Amerian dream raising babies, chickens, and baking delicious pies!
